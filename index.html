About Dusukure Inkambi

Dusukure Inkambi was founded by Melvin after witnessing severe pollution and environmental harm in the refugee camp where he lived with his family. Determined to create change, he took action, leading youth-driven efforts to clean camps, educate communities, and expand environmental initiatives across Rwanda.

We believe that everyone--regardless of their circumstances--deserves to live in a clean, dignified space. By prioritizing cleanliness and waste management, we not only prevent disease outbreaks and enhance the mental well-being of camp residents but also contribute to a greener planet for future generations.

Our Mission & Vision

Guided by purpose and compassion

Our Mission

Dusukure Inkambi provides free sanitation services to refugee camps and underprivileged regions in Rwanda, restoring dignity, improving public health, and promoting environmental sustainability for a cleaner, greener future for our Planet.

Our Vision

A cleaner, healthier Rwanda where every refugee camp and underprivileged community thrives in a safe, dignified, and sustainable environment, fostering a greener planet for future generations.

Join Our Mission
